## Time of the $$k^\text{th}$$ Arrival

$$Y_k=T_1+\ldots+T_k$$

$$p_{Y_k}(t)=\binom{t-1}{k-1}p^k(1-p)^{t-k},\qquad t=k,k+1,\ldots$$
